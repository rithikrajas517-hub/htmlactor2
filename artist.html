vj siddhu

VJ Siddhu is a prominent Tamil video jockey and YouTuber, widely recognized for his engaging prank shows and social service-based content. He gained significant popularity through his prank show "Fun Panrom" on the Black Sheep YouTube channel, which garnered him a substantial fan base. Siddhu also hosted the social service-oriented show "Sindhanai Sei With Siddhu." Siddhu made his television debut in 2019 with the reality game show "Killadi Rani" on Jaya TV. His YouTube channel, VJ Siddhu Vlogs, has also been well-received, with several of his videos amassing millions of views. Born in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu, Siddhu completed his schooling at Little Flower Higher Secondary School and pursued higher education at EGS Pillay Engineering College and Anna University. He is married to Punitha Shalini M., and they have a daughter named Sidshals Iniyaazh, who is also a popular YouTuber with her channel "Ini Vlogs"​.
